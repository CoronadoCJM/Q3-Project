BLACKPINK

BLACKPINK is a four member K-pop girl group under YG Entertainment. Comprised of members Jennie, Jisoo, Lisa and Rosé, the group debuted — after perfecting their craft for several years in a traditonal K-pop trainee program — in 2016 with the songs “Whistle" and "Boombayah

They made history at the Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival as the first K-pop band to headline the event

The ladies arrived in style for their headlining set Saturday night. Their stunning outfits that they donned while performing in front of what looked like a large temple. They kicked off the evening with “Pink Venom," a hit off their latest album, "Born Pink."

Throughout the years, BLACKPINK has released various albums and singles that helped push the band into international stardom.
